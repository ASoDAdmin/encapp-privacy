1. About this policy

Encapp is a business tool for field energy and lighting auditors. It is provided to professionals by their organisation. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and your choices. Encapp is not directed at children.

2. Data we collect

We only collect what the app needs to do its job:

We do not collect: location, contacts, browsing history, advertising identifiers, biometric data, or any analytics/usage telemetry. The app contains no third‑party tracking, analytics, advertising, or crash‑reporting SDKs.

3. How we use data

Solely to provide the app's functionality: authenticate you, store and sync your audit work, generate reports/exports, and keep your data consistent across your devices. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for their own purposes.

4. Where data goes

Your audit data and photos are stored on your device first and synced to your organisation's own secure backend hosted on Microsoft Azure. Transmission is encrypted in transit over HTTPS. Azure acts as our infrastructure/hosting provider (a processor); it does not use your data for its own purposes. No other third parties receive your data.

5. Data retention & deletion

Audit data belongs to the auditing organisation that operates Encapp. Because this is a business app, requests to access, correct, or delete data are handled through that organisation / the account owner. To make a request, contact mj@qdsl.io or your organisation's Encapp administrator, who can remove your account and associated records from the backend. Data cached on a device is removed when you sign out or uninstall the app.

6. Device permissions

  • Camera — to photograph sites, rooms, and assets for audit records.
  • Microphone — to record audio or video evidence for audit records, when you use that feature.
  • Photos / media — to attach existing images and to save exported images.

These permissions are requested in context and used only for the purposes above. You can revoke them in your device settings; some capture features will then be unavailable.

7. Security

We use platform secure storage for credentials (Keychain/Keystore), HTTPS for all network traffic, and authenticated, organisation‑scoped access to the backend. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures appropriate to the sensitivity of business audit data.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g. the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the GDPR, or other applicable privacy law), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Exercise these via mj@qdsl.io or your organisation's administrator.
